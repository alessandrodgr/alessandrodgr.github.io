About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Compositional Systems and Methods group at Tallinn University of Technology.

Before that, I was a research fellow in the Principles of Programming, Logic and Verification group at University College London.

I obtained my PhD from the University of Pisa, under the supervision of Prof. Filippo Bonchi.


Research interests

  • Programming language semantics
  • Categorical semantics
  • String diagrams
  • Logic
